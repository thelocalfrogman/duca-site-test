## Semester 2 Kickoff BBQ

We kicked off Semester 2 2025 with our traditional welcome BBQ! Over 80 students joined us for an afternoon of food, fun, and cybersecurity conversations.

### Event Recap

- **Attendance**: 80+ students (new record!)
- **New Members**: 35 students joined DUCA
- **Food**: Burgers, sausages, veggie options, and refreshments

### Activities

- Meet & greet with DUCA committee members
- CTF mini-challenges with prizes
- Division showcase presentations
- Networking opportunities

### What's Next

We outlined our plans for the semester including:
- Weekly workshops
- Monthly social events
- CTF team training
- Industry guest speakers

Thank you to everyone who came out and made this event a success! Looking forward to an amazing semester together.
