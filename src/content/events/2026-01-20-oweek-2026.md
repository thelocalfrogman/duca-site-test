## O-Week 2026

DUCA had an amazing presence at O-Week 2026! Our stall was one of the busiest, with hundreds of students stopping by to learn about cybersecurity and club activities.

### By the Numbers

- **500+** students visited our stall
- **200+** new Discord members
- **150+** club sign-ups
- **5** days of outreach

### What We Offered

- Live hacking demonstrations
- CTF challenge station
- Free DUCA stickers and merchandise
- Information about upcoming events
- Meet the committee members

### Highlights

Our CTF challenge station was a huge hit! Students got to try their hand at beginner-friendly challenges and win prizes. Many discovered their interest in cybersecurity through these interactive demos.

### Thank You!

A huge thank you to our amazing volunteers who ran the stall throughout the week. Your enthusiasm and dedication helped us reach more students than ever before.

Welcome to all our new members - we can't wait to see you at our upcoming events!
