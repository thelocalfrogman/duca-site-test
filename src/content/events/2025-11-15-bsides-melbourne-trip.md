## BSides Melbourne 2025

DUCA organized a group trip to BSides Melbourne 2025, where our members had the opportunity to attend talks, participate in workshops, and network with industry professionals.

### Event Highlights

- Keynote on emerging threats in cloud security
- Hands-on hardware hacking village
- CTF competition (DUCA team placed in top 10!)
- Networking with security professionals from across Australia

### What Our Members Said

> "My first security conference and it was amazing! The energy in the room was incredible." - First-year member

> "Great opportunity to see what the industry is really like outside of university." - Third-year member
