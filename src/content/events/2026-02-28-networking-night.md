## Networking Night

This is your chance to meet like-minded students, industry professionals, and DUCA alumni who are passionate about cybersecurity.

### What to Expect

- **Industry Guests**: Representatives from leading cybersecurity companies
- **Alumni Connections**: Meet former DUCA members now working in the field
- **Peer Networking**: Connect with students from all year levels
- **Career Insights**: Learn about internship and job opportunities

### Why Attend?

Building your professional network is crucial in the cybersecurity industry. This event provides a relaxed environment to:

- Exchange ideas and experiences
- Learn about different career paths
- Find potential mentors
- Discover internship opportunities
- Make lasting connections
