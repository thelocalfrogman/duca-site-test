## Ethical Hacking 101

Ever wondered what ethical hackers do? This workshop will introduce you to the exciting world of penetration testing and security assessments.

### Topics Covered

1. **Introduction to Ethical Hacking**
   - What is penetration testing?
   - Legal and ethical considerations
   - Career opportunities in offensive security

2. **Penetration Testing Methodology**
   - Reconnaissance and information gathering
   - Vulnerability assessment
   - Exploitation basics
   - Reporting and documentation

3. **Essential Tools**
   - Kali Linux overview
   - Nmap for network scanning
   - Burp Suite for web testing
   - Metasploit basics

4. **Hands-on Lab**
   - Practice on vulnerable virtual machines
   - Real-world scenario simulation

### Prerequisites

- Basic understanding of networking concepts
- Laptop with VirtualBox or VMware installed
- Kali Linux VM (instructions will be provided before the event)

### Important Notes

- All activities will be conducted in isolated lab environments
- Participants must agree to ethical hacking guidelines
- This workshop is for educational purposes only

Join us for an engaging session that could kickstart your journey into offensive security!
