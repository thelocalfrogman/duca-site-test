## What You'll Learn

Join us for an exciting introduction to CTF (Capture The Flag) competitions! This beginner-friendly workshop will cover:

- What are CTF competitions?
- Different categories of challenges (Web, Crypto, Forensics, etc.)
- Tools and resources to get started
- Hands-on practice with beginner challenges

## Who Should Attend

This workshop is perfect for:
- Complete beginners to cybersecurity
- Students curious about ethical hacking
- Anyone wanting to join DUCA's CTF team

## What to Bring

- Laptop with Kali Linux VM installed (or we can help you set it up!)
- Enthusiasm to learn!

No prior experience required. All skill levels welcome!
