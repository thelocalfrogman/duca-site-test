## Ransomware Simulation Workshop

This workshop provided a safe environment to experience and respond to a simulated ransomware attack, teaching crucial incident response skills.

### What Happened

Participants were divided into teams and faced a realistic ransomware scenario:

1. **Initial Compromise**: Teams discovered their systems had been encrypted
2. **Incident Response**: Following proper IR procedures to contain the threat
3. **Investigation**: Analyzing how the attack occurred
4. **Recovery**: Restoring systems from backups
5. **Lessons Learned**: Post-incident review and improvements

### Key Takeaways

- Importance of regular backups and testing recovery procedures
- Incident response plan development
- Communication during security incidents
- Technical indicators of ransomware attacks

### Participant Feedback

The workshop received excellent feedback, with 95% of participants rating it as "highly valuable" for their learning.
