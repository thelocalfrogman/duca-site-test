## Cyber Careers Panel Discussion

Join us for an insightful panel discussion featuring cybersecurity professionals from various sectors of the industry.

### Our Panelists

We've invited professionals from diverse backgrounds including:

- Security Operations Center (SOC) Analyst
- Penetration Tester
- Security Consultant
- Incident Response Specialist
- GRC (Governance, Risk, Compliance) Professional

### Discussion Topics

- How they got started in cybersecurity
- Day-to-day responsibilities
- Required skills and certifications
- Industry trends and future outlook
- Tips for landing your first security role

### Q&A Session

The panel will be followed by an open Q&A session where you can ask specific questions about:

- Career paths
- Certifications (OSCP, CISSP, CEH, etc.)
- Interview preparation
- Building a portfolio
